Georgia power grid

What the data says

  • Oil & gas is the largest source at 46.8% of operating capacity.

  • The five largest mapped plants represent 36.5% of statewide operating capacity.
  • Data-center coverage includes 19 community-mapped locations and should not be treated as a complete inventory.
