# Attribution

US Grid Explorer combines original code and analysis with public and third-party datasets. The application should not be treated as an official government product.

## Map Data And Basemaps

- OpenStreetMap contributors: map and community-reported data-center information. OpenStreetMap data is available under the Open Database License. See https://www.openstreetmap.org/copyright.
- CARTO basemap tiles are displayed with on-map attribution where used.
- Leaflet is used for interactive mapping.

## Power Grid And Energy Data

- U.S. Energy Information Administration, Form EIA-860: power plant, generator, capacity, fuel, operator, and location records. See https://www.eia.gov/electricity/data/eia860/.
- U.S. Energy Information Administration, EIA-930 Hourly Electric Grid Monitor: balancing-authority demand signals. See https://www.eia.gov/electricity/gridmonitor/about.

## Infrastructure Layers

- Public ArcGIS FeatureServer services are used for transmission-line viewport queries.
- HIFLD / Oak Ridge National Laboratory and partner agencies are referenced for substation source metadata.
- Esri ArcGIS World Geocoding Service is used for explicit user-submitted geographic search.

## Public Notices

- Federal Register API records are used for the Data Center Watch feed. Records are linked to their original Federal Register pages and are summarized for navigation and discovery.
