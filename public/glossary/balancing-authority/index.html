Grid operations · Definition

Balancing authority

The responsible entity that continuously balances electricity supply, demand, and interchange within a defined area.

In plain languageThe responsible entity that continuously balances electricity supply, demand, and interchange within a defined area.
Category
Grid operations
Primary reference
North American Electric Reliability Corporation

Why it matters

How this term helps you read grid data

Electricity must remain balanced in real time. A balancing authority coordinates resources and neighboring systems when demand or generation changes.

Example

Balancing authority in practice

If demand rises unexpectedly, the balancing authority must arrange additional supply or reduce exports to keep the system balanced.
