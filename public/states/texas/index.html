TX · State grid profile

Texas power grid

Texas operates the country's largest state power system by nameplate capacity in this dataset. Natural gas remains its largest resource while wind, solar, and storage make the state one of the most varied generation portfolios to explore.

What the data says

  • Oil & gas is the largest source at 46.2% of operating capacity.
  • The state reports 450 proposed generators totaling 88,939 MW.
  • The five largest mapped plants represent 7.5% of statewide operating capacity.
  • Data-center coverage includes 52 community-mapped locations and should not be treated as a complete inventory.

Methodology
