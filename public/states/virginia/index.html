Virginia power grid

What the data says

  • Oil & gas is the largest source at 52.2% of operating capacity.
  • The state reports 63 proposed generators totaling 7,714 MW.
  • The five largest mapped plants represent 31.7% of statewide operating capacity.
  • Data-center coverage includes 154 community-mapped locations and should not be treated as a complete inventory.
